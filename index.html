IRENE K


Irene is one of the many brave essential workers in St. Louis, MO who risks her life every single day to keep thousands of Americans in her area safe.

Although she treats non-COVID-19 patients, she witnesses the struggle of those infected on a daily basis. Irene’s position still puts her in an immense amount of danger, because she frequently comes into close contact with those involved in the battle against COVID-19. The essential work of this nurse is best embodied through this quote: “Everyday, I walk to hospital floors with fear for not only my health, but the health of my family and loved ones who come into contact with me. I feel the pain of the families losing loved ones. Seeing death occur never gets easier, it’s painful to watch every time. However, I find strength and hope in the work that I am doing, because I am essential to stopping the spread.” Countless nurses all around the world expose themselves to these risks and follow their passion for helping others.

Irene stresses the need for mental health, as quarantine and various other sources of upheaval within our country can seriously affect our mental stabilities. To stay clear headed, she recommends that we interact with the ones we love, whether it be in a s afe manner or via technology. Following a routine and volunteering your time to support efforts to end the pandemic can really fill you with appreciation for the essential work of these frontliners. Learning something new can also be an effective way to keep your mind sharp.

Her story is a testament to what we can do as individuals to aid those in need. Out of consideration for others, you should wear a mask and stay home if you aren’t feeling well.

OUR MISSION


Our Founders, Allan Manzano and Michael Osborne, created the #iamessential.campaign in the midst of a raging pandemic. Out of the chaos, they put forward the novel idea of sourcing masks and delivering them to fronliners. Our organization donates 100% of the funds we raise to assist the brave women and men who risk their lives each day. The work we do goes a long way in recognizing the risks that these incredible people take to keep us safe and healthy. In addition, because our movement donates masks at no costs, we have no borders to our efforts. Communities all over the world deserve to get the proper medical coverage, regardless of economic status or race, so we strive to be inclusive. Our non-preferential model has already reached regions as far as the Middle East. We volunteer our time and effort to give frontliners and the disenfranchised a voice in an often drowning world.


THE ESSENTIAL PROJECT TEAM


As four students from around the United States, we formed The Essential Project the umbrella of iamessential.campaign. Our efforts focus on boosting the accessibility to and awareness of our movement. We are passionate about making real change in the world, so we put our creative and professional skills together to make communities safer. We have already seen the impact of our work in the areas that we live and hope to take it to the next level with your help.
